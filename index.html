Ransomware Protection

Our Clients Don't Pay Ransoms. Period.

Ransomware is the number one threat to small businesses today. The average attack costs over $150,000 in downtime, lost data, and recovery — and many businesses never fully recover. But it doesn't have to be that way.

🛡

100% Track Record

Every client that follows our security recommendations has been completely protected from ransomware. Not "mostly protected." Not "we minimized the damage." One hundred percent. Zero successful attacks. Zero ransoms paid. Zero data lost.

🔒

Defense in Depth

There's no single magic product that stops ransomware. We build layered defenses — proper firewalls, network segmentation, access controls, patching, and endpoint protection — so attackers can't get a foothold in the first place.

💾

Bulletproof Backups

Even if the worst happens, our backup strategy means you're back up and running — not negotiating with criminals. Immutable snapshots, offsite copies, and tested restores. Ransomware can't encrypt what it can't reach.

Most businesses don't find out their ransomware plan doesn't work until it's too late. We'd rather show you the gaps now — before someone else finds them for you.
