Networks & Security

If You Don't Know Whether You're Secure, You're Probably Not

Most small businesses we meet have one thing in common: nobody's really sure what's protecting their network. There's a router from the internet company, maybe a firewall somebody set up once, and a lot of fingers crossed.

We fix that. We set up proper network security that protects your business without making everyone's life harder.

  • Firewall setup and management using pfSense (powerful, free, and trusted by thousands of businesses)
  • Network segmentation — keeping your sensitive data separate from guest Wi-Fi and IoT devices
  • VPN access for remote workers — secure connections from anywhere
  • Intrusion detection — knowing when someone's trying to get in
  • Wi-Fi that actually works — proper access points, not consumer routers daisy-chained together
  • Regular security reviews — because threats evolve and your defenses should too
Real Talk

Security Doesn't Have to Be Complicated

We're not going to scare you into buying a bunch of expensive security products you don't need. Most small businesses need the fundamentals done well:

A real firewall. Not a consumer router. A proper firewall that controls what goes in and out of your network. We use pfSense — the same caliber of firewall that large companies use, without the large company price tag.

Segmented networks. Your point-of-sale system shouldn't be on the same network as your guest Wi-Fi. Your security cameras shouldn't be accessible from the internet. Basic stuff, but most small businesses don't have it.

Regular updates. Unpatched servers are the number one way businesses get compromised. We keep everything current so you don't have to think about it.

Storage & Backups

Your Data Is Your Business. Treat It That Way.

Ask yourself: if your server died right now, how long would it take to get back to normal? If the answer is "I don't know" or "a really long time," we need to talk.

We set up storage and backup systems that actually work when you need them — because the worst time to find out your backups are broken is when you're trying to restore from them.

  • Centralized file storage using TrueNAS — reliable, fast, and built for business
  • Automated backups that run without anyone babysitting them
  • Backup testing — we regularly verify your backups actually restore
  • Offsite copies — because a backup in the same building as your server isn't much help in a fire
  • Fast recovery — get back to work in hours, not days
  • File sharing that works for your team — Windows, Mac, whatever you use
The Right Foundation

Storage That Protects Itself

We build storage systems on TrueNAS with ZFS — a technology specifically designed to protect your data from corruption, hardware failure, and human error.

Unlike traditional server storage, ZFS constantly checks your data for errors and fixes them automatically. It takes instant snapshots so you can recover accidentally deleted files in seconds. And it does all of this without expensive add-on software.

Combined with our backup strategy — local snapshots, automated backups, and offsite copies — your business data is protected at every level.
